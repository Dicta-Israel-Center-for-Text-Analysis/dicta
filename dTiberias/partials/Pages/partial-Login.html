What does DICTA do?

Eliminate transcription errors in a text

Vocalize and punctuate a text, disambiguate abbreviations

Tag words for morphology

Label sentences for rhetorical function

Mark references in text and identify them

For anonymous texts, identify period/region or author

For multi-author texts, identify authorial/editorial layers*

For every passage in text, find all parallel passages in a selected library*

Given two libraries, find linguistic markers of each, assign new texts to right library*

Enhanced search, inc. synonyms, co-citations, morphological features
