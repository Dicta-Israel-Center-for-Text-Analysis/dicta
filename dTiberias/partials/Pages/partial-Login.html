Corpus Construction

Assemble a comprehensive corpus of Jewish literature in a single format.

Annotate documents (genre/period/region/author), paragraphs and words (morphology).

Standardize orthography (open abbreviations, maleh/chaser, etc.)

Permit searching utilizing any combination of linguistic features and annotations.

Authorship Analysis

Automatically identify the author of an anonymous document.

Alternatively, identify period and region of an anonymous document.

Determine the probability that two documents were written by the same author.

Automatically decompose a multi-author document into authorial threads.

Intertextuality

Automatically identify citations and their referents.

Identify text reuse across books (including paraphrases) even in huge corpora.

Analyze the citation/paraphrase matrix to track the flow of information over time.

Automatically identify the central sources (hubs and authorities) for any selected topic.

Integrate the above with crowdsourcing methods.

Ur-text Reconstruction

Automatically align multiple textual witnesses into a synoptic text.

Reconstruct the maximum likelihood stemma of textual witnesses for a given text.

Automatically reconstruct the ur-text from which textual witnesses evolved.
